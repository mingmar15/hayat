Our Story

A Legacy Rooted in
Himalayan Hospitality

Hayat Hotel was born from a vision — to create a space where the ancient spiritual energy of Boudhanath and the comforts of modern luxury coexist harmoniously. Founded in 2013, we have since become one of Kathmandu's most beloved retreats.

Our hotel stands just 200 metres from the magnificent Boudhanath Stupa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offering guests immediate access to one of the largest Buddhist stupas in the world.
